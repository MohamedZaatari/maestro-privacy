Maestro AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

Maestro AI ("we", "us") provides an AI-powered social media management service that businesses use to create, publish, and manage content and engagement on their own social media accounts. This policy explains what data we access and how we use it.

What we access

When a business connects its social media account (e.g. an Instagram professional account, a Facebook Page, or a TikTok account) to Maestro AI, and only with that business's authorization, we may access:

  • The business's own profile information and media (posts, images, videos).
  • Comments on the business's own posts.
  • Direct messages sent to the business's account.
  • Insights/analytics for the business's own account and media.

We access this data solely to provide the service to that business — publishing their content, replying to their customers on their behalf, and reporting their performance.

How we use data

  • To publish content the business creates or approves.
  • To read and reply to comments and messages on the business's behalf, in the business's brand voice.
  • To generate reports/insights for the business owner.

We do not sell personal data, and we do not use it for advertising to third parties.

Data sharing

We use trusted service providers to operate Maestro AI, including Meta Platforms (for Instagram/Facebook access), TikTok (for TikTok content publishing), Anthropic (to generate content and replies), and cloud infrastructure providers. These providers process data only to deliver our service.

Data retention & deletion

We retain connected-account data only as long as needed to provide the service. A business may disconnect its account at any time, and may request deletion of its data by contacting us at the email below; we will delete it promptly, subject to legal requirements.

Your rights

Businesses and their users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their data. To exercise these rights, contact us.
